KBZ Mobile Banking (Individual) Unauthorized Withdrawl

A user reported that 189 lakhs was transferred from their KBZ Mobile Banking account to an unknown account (WAI ZAW KO KO, Acc No : 06930106902829001) without their authorization. The user was overseas and had only shared their SIM card with their spouse for reeiving OTP codes when needed. However,no OTP was received for this transaction. The user has contacted KBZ Bank's customer service and filled a police report, but was informed that the money has already been withdrawn from the recipient's account.

Read more

Prevention Tips:

  • Never share your mobile banking credential with anyone.
  • Enable biometric authentication if available.
  • Regularly check your account for unauthorized transactions.
  • Contact your bank immediately if you notice suspicious activity.
